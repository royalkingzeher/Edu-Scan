Title: Let's Learn Addition: Adding Two Numbers

Introduction:
Welcome to an exciting journey into the world of addition! In Class 1, we begin to explore the fascinating realm of mathematics, starting with the basic concept of addition. Today, we'll discover how to add two numbers together to find their total.


Understanding Addition:
Addition is like putting things together to find out how many there are in total. Imagine you have some apples and someone gives you more. When you put them together, you count how many apples you have in all. The numbers we add are called "addends," and the answer we get is called the "sum."


Properties of Addition:
Adding numbers follows some simple rules.


1. The order doesn't matter! For example, adding 2 + 3 is the same as adding 3 + 2. Both give us the answer of 5.
2. We can group numbers differently and still get the same answer. So, if we have (1 + 2) + 3, it's the same as 1 + (2 + 3), both equaling 6.
